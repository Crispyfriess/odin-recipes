Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is packed with layers of seasoned beef and pork, rich tomato sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella. It is the kind of dish that takes some time to make but is absolutely worth every minute.

Best made ahead of time and reheated — the flavours get even better the next day.

Ingredients

  • 450g sweet Italian sausage
  • 340g lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes (800g)
  • 2 cans tomato paste (170g each)
  • 2 cans tomato sauce (190g each)
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1½ teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 475g ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 900g mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a large pot over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water.
  3. Season with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and 2 tablespoons of parsley.
  4. Simmer on low heat, covered, for about 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally.
  5. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
  6. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  7. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, remaining parsley, and ½ teaspoon salt.
  8. Spread 1.5 cups of meat sauce on the bottom of a 23x33cm baking dish.
  9. Layer 6 noodles on top, then spread half the ricotta mixture, half the mozzarella slices, and a third of the meat sauce.
  10. Repeat the layers — noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and meat sauce.
  11. Top with remaining noodles, meat sauce, mozzarella, and sprinkle Parmesan on top.
  12. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove foil and bake for another 25 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  14. Let it rest for 15 minutes before serving.
